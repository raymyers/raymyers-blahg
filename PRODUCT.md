# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Programmers who read personal technical blogs for thoughtful, opinionated writing. They are likely interested in software quality, refactoring, AI skepticism, formal methods, and the human side of programming.

## Product Purpose
This site is Ray Myers' personal blog and web home. It should give programmers amusing, curious, and intellectually fulfilling essays, talks, and links, while making the author's perspective easy to recognize and follow.

## Brand Personality
Wry, curious, rigorous.

## Anti-references
The site should not feel trendy, growth-hacked, SaaS-like, influencer-coded, or visually optimized for whatever design pattern is currently fashionable. Avoid generic developer-blog tropes that feel like a template wearing a technical costume.

## Design Principles
- Let the writing lead; visual choices should frame the voice, not compete with it.
- Prefer durable, slightly odd character over trend alignment.
- Make curiosity easy: posts, talks, tags, and related paths should invite browsing.
- Keep the tone intellectually serious without becoming self-important.
- Preserve the handmade personal-site feeling while improving clarity and polish.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
Target WCAG AA for contrast, keyboard access, readable type, and reduced-motion support. Preserve light and dark theme support.
